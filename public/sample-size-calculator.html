How this works

n = ( zα/2 × √(2 × p̄ × (1−p̄)) + zβ × √(p₀×(1−p₀) + p₁×(1−p₁)) )² / (p₁ − p₀)² where: p₀ = baseline conversion rate p₁ = p₀ + expected lift p̄ = (p₀ + p₁) / 2 zα/2 = normal quantile for significance level (1.96 for α=0.05) zβ = normal quantile for power (0.842 for 80% power)
