Trade-offs & Decisions

What was built vs. cut — and what does that reveal about priorities?

权衡与决策

做了什么，放弃了什么——这揭示了什么样的优先级？

Every product decision is a seesaw

Product decisions are made under real constraints — limited time, real API costs, a single developer, specific use cases in mind. Every choice pushes one side of the seesaw up and the other down. A PM's job isn't to judge these choices — it's to understand what they reveal about priorities.

每个产品决策都是一个跷跷板

产品决策在真实约束下做出——时间有限、API成本真实存在、只有一个开发者、心里有特定的使用场景。每个选择都让跷跷板一端上升，另一端下降。产品经理的工作不是评判这些选择的对错——而是理解它们揭示了什么优先级。

Decision 4: LLM-agnostic architecture

决策四：大语言模型无关的架构

Decision决策 Isolate AI to reasoning only — tools layer has zero LLM dependency 将 AI 限定于推理层——工具层对大语言模型零依赖
Option方案Gain获得Give Up放弃Status状态
LLM-agnostic design大模型无关设计 Swap Claude for any model without changing business logic. Avoids vendor lock-in.无需修改业务逻辑即可替换 Claude。避免供应商锁定。 More architectural discipline. Can't use LLM-specific features in the tools layer.需要更严格的架构规范。工具层无法使用特定大模型的专属功能。 ✓ Chosen已采用
Tightly coupled LLM与大模型紧耦合 Easier to write initially.初期开发更简单。 Hard to switch models. Non-deterministic behavior in data layer. Harder to test.难以更换模型。数据层行为不确定。更难测试。 Not built未采用
⚠️ A PM Would Flag This Risk 产品经理会标记这个风险
The single-user design is the product's biggest strategic constraint. No auth, no multi-tenancy — it can't be monetized, can't grow through word-of-mouth, can't scale without a rebuild. A PM planning the next phase would flag this as the primary architectural blocker to any future growth.
单用户设计是产品最大的战略约束。没有认证机制，没有多租户支持——无法变现，无法通过口碑增长，不重构就无法规模化。规划下一阶段的产品经理会将其标记为未来增长的首要架构障碍。
