A job search is a volume game

Think of a flywheel. The more jobs you apply to, the faster you learn what works. The faster you can apply, the more jobs you try. The product sits at the center of this loop — keeping the wheel spinning without burning you out.

求职是一场数量游戏

想象一个飞轮。你投递的职位越多，你就越快知道什么有效。申请速度越快，你就能尝试更多机会。这款产品处于这个循环的中心——让飞轮持续转动，而不把你耗尽。

What brings users back

Strong products have a retention hook. For this product, the hook is history — every analysis is auto-saved. Over weeks of job searching, users accumulate a record of every role analyzed, every match score, every application made.

是什么让用户回头

强产品都有留存钩子。这款产品的钩子是历史记录——每次分析都自动保存。随着求职周期延长，用户积累了每个分析过的职位、每次匹配分数、每次申请记录。
